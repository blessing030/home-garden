    How to prepare a sumptuous pilau
  • Heat oil in a large pot and fry onions until golden brown
  • Add the garlic, ginger, and spices and stir for a few seconds until fragrant
  • Add the meat (if using) and cook until browned
  • Add the tomatoes and cook until soft
  • Stir in the potatoes and rice, mixing well with the spices
  • Pour in the water or broth, add salt, and bring to a boil
  • Cover, reduce heat to low, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed
  • Fluff with a fork and serve hot with kachumbari (tomato & onion salad) or yogurt or banana
